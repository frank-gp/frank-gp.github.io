Placeholder Syntax:

  • $1 - Simple tab stop
  • ${1|option1,option2|} - Choice selection
  • $0 - Final cursor position
  • ${CLIPBOARD} - Clipboard content
  • ${TM_SELECTED_TEXT} - Selected text
